# 💳 Credit Card Financial Dashboard

An end-to-end **Credit Card Business Analytics Project** that analyzes transaction data, customer behavior, and revenue drivers to generate actionable insights for financial institutions. This project focuses on building an **interactive dashboard** to monitor weekly credit card performance, customer segmentation, and spending patterns.

---

# 📊 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to develop a **comprehensive credit card analytics dashboard** that provides insights into key business metrics such as:

- Revenue generation
- Customer behavior
- Transaction trends
- Weekly performance monitoring

The dashboard helps stakeholders **analyze credit card operations and support data-driven decision-making.**

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 💰 Interest is the Primary Revenue Driver
- Interest contributes **72% of total revenue**
- Indicates strong reliance on **revolving balances**

### 💳 Blue Cards Generate the Most Revenue
- Blue cards contribute the largest revenue share
- Indicates majority of customers use **entry-level credit cards**

### 🛒 Bills Are the Most Common Spending Category
- Customers frequently use credit cards for **regular household payments**

### 👥 High-Income Customers Drive More Revenue
- Higher income segments contribute significantly to revenue generation

### 🎯 Middle-Aged Customers Are the Most Valuable Segment
- Customers aged **40–50 generate the highest revenue**

### 💳 Swipe Transactions Dominate
- Physical card transactions are still more common than online payments

# 🚀 Recommendations

Based on the analysis, several business recommendations were identified:

### 🎯 Target High-Income Customers
Focus marketing efforts on high-income professionals who generate the highest revenue.

### 💳 Promote Premium Card Upgrades
Encourage customers with high spending behavior to upgrade to **Gold or Platinum cards**.

### 📱 Encourage Digital Transactions
Promote online and contactless payments through cashback offers and rewards.

### ⭐ Improve Customer Satisfaction
Enhance customer experience through better support, rewards, and personalized offers.

### ⚠ Strengthen Credit Risk Monitoring
Monitor delinquency rates and credit utilization ratios to prevent financial losses.
